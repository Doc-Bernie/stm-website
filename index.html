Why Social Tennis Manager?

Less admin. More tennis.

STM helps you move from WhatsApp chaos and payment headaches — to fully managed sessions with players confirmed, payments collected and draws ready to go.

01
Set up in minutes

Organisers and coaches create a session in minutes — recreate from a template each week.

02
No more chat group chaos

Stop chasing replies on WhatsApp. The roster is in the app — clean, current, and visible to everyone.

03
Players join with ease

One tap to join the main list or waitlist. Players see who else is playing and confirm in seconds.

04
You're in control

Choose how you run things — payments, credits, draws, cancellations. Turn features on or off to match your club.

05
Built for your club and tennis community

Customisable session formats, member levels, pricing rules and more. Your club, your way.

06
Free to start, free forever

The free version covers most of what you need to run sessions. Upgrade only if you want the extras.

Getting Started

Create your
first session
in minutes.

New to STM? This 2-minute walkthrough shows you exactly how to set up your first session, from opening the admin dashboard to sharing your Club ID with players.

  • Open your Admin Dashboard and tap Create Session
  • Fill in the name, date, time and venue
  • Set spots, price, and toggle player level & payment options
  • Hit Save — your session is instantly live for players
