Section 01

About Social Tennis Manager

STM is a session management application designed to help tennis organisers, coaches and clubs run their sessions more efficiently. The App enables organisers to:

  • Create and manage tennis sessions
  • Manage player rosters, waitlists and member records
  • Build and publish match draws
  • Collect payments from players via Stripe Connect
  • Issue session credits when sessions are cancelled
  • Communicate with players through push notifications

STM is a software platform. We facilitate the connection between organisers and players but do not organise, supervise, insure, endorse or guarantee any tennis session, match or interaction between users.

Section 02

User Roles

The App supports three primary user roles, each with different responsibilities:

  • Organisers (Admins) — operators of clubs or session groups who create and manage sessions, set pricing, and receive payments.
  • Members — registered players associated with a specific club, with verified accounts and access to member-specific features.
  • Players (Guests) — non-registered participants who join individual sessions without creating a member account.

Organisers are independent operators of their own tennis activities. STM is not a party to the relationship between an organiser and their players, and is not responsible for the conduct, decisions or services provided by organisers.

Section 07

Session Credits and Cancellations

STM includes a session credit feature that automatically converts paid session fees into credits when a session is cancelled by the organiser.

  • Credits are not refunds — when a session is cancelled, the organiser retains the funds and the player receives a credit equal to what they paid, redeemable against future sessions in the same club.
  • Organiser responsibility — by using this feature, organisers commit to honouring credits in good faith for future sessions.
  • Player rights — players who believe a credit has been improperly withheld should first contact the organiser. STM may assist in resolving disputes but is not a party to the underlying transaction.
